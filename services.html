Core Services OFCS Can Offer the Community

Below is a detailed look at the ways OFCS supports Filipino-Canadians and our wider community throughout the year.

1) Community Connection & Welcome Services

  • Newcomer orientation & referral support — Helping newly arrived Filipino-Canadians, TFWs, and international students connect to settlement services, housing resources, language supports, and local community groups.
  • Social meetups & community networking sessions — Ongoing opportunities to meet, share experiences, and build meaningful connections.

2) Cultural Events & Activities

  • Annual cultural celebrations — Philippine Independence Day, Sinulog Festival, Simbang Gabi, Christmas gatherings, and more.
  • Arts, dance, and heritage showcases — Performances and cultural features that highlight Filipino talent and tradition.
  • Film nights & cultural workshops — Traditional craft workshops and cultural education sessions that bring Filipino culture to a broader audience.

3) Workforce & Professional Development

  • Career talks & job readiness workshops
  • Résumé writing and interview clinics
  • Mentorship for youth and young professionals — Confidence-building support and practical guidance for career growth.

4) Youth & Leadership Programs

  • Quarterly Youth & Young Professionals Month
  • Leadership development & volunteer training
  • Peer mentorship circles & networking events — Helping the next generation build community leadership and connection.

5) General Inquiries & Guidance

  • Community support helpdesk — A place to ask questions and get referred to appropriate services.
  • Information sessions — Topics like settlement, legal rights, healthcare access, and educational planning.

6) Partnership & Outreach Support

  • Partnerships with local organizations — Collaborations with other cultural groups, non-profits, and government programs.
  • Consulate outreach support — Coordination with the Philippine Consulate for outreach missions (passport services, dual citizenship, etc.).

7) Wellness & Support Initiatives

  • Mental health & inclusion workshops
  • Support circles — For families, seniors, and caregivers.
  • Volunteer recognition & community care events — Strengthening belonging, resilience, and connection.
