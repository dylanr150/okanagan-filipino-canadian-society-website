Okanagan Filipino-Canadian Society

Rooted in Culture. United in Community.

Celebrating 30 years of serving Filipino-Canadians in the Okanagan, the Okanagan Filipino-Canadian Society (OFCS) connects people through culture, service, and bayanihan—building a strong, inclusive community for generations past, present, and future.

30 Years of Community, Culture, and Connection

For three decades, OFCS has proudly supported Filipino-Canadians across the Okanagan—celebrating culture, welcoming newcomers, empowering youth, and fostering lifelong community connections through service and shared heritage.

What 2026 Looks Like

Our 2026 programming reflects the heart of OFCS—culture, youth, family, wellness, and community. While dates will be announced soon and programs may evolve, here’s an at-a-glance look at what the year ahead has in store.

Culture & Heritage

Community celebrations, heritage showcases, and events that keep our traditions thriving.

Youth & Leadership

Youth programming and mentorship that builds confidence, skills, and community pride.

Family & Community

Gatherings and initiatives that bring families together and strengthen local connections.

Wellness & Support

Workshops and resources that support wellbeing, inclusion, and a sense of belonging.
