About Us

Building community for Filipino Canadians in the Okanagan

The Okanagan Filipino-Canadian Society (OFCS) is a community organization focused on connection, culture, and support. We bring people together through events, outreach, and community-building initiatives.

Our Mission

The Okanagan Filipino-Canadian Society (OFCS) is dedicated to supporting, empowering, and uniting the Filipino community in the Okanagan through cultural preservation, community service, newcomer support, and meaningful partnerships. We serve as a trusted bridge that connects Filipinos to resources, opportunities, and one another while celebrating our heritage and contributing positively to the broader Canadian community.

Our Vision

We envision a strong, visible, and connected Filipino-Canadian community in the Okanagan — one that is proud of its cultural roots, supported through every stage of settlement, engaged across generations, and recognized as an active and valued contributor to the region’s social, cultural, and economic life.

What We Do

  • Host cultural events, gatherings, and celebrations
  • Share updates, resources, and community information
  • Collaborate with local groups and community partners

Our Values

These values guide how we show up for our members and the wider community.
