About Us

Building community for Filipino Canadians in the Okanagan

Okanagan Filipino Canadians is a community organization focused on connection, culture, and support. We bring people together through events, outreach, and community-building initiatives.

Our Mission

To strengthen Filipino-Canadian community in the Okanagan by creating welcoming spaces, supporting newcomers and families, and celebrating Filipino culture and heritage.

Our Vision

A connected, thriving Filipino-Canadian community where people feel supported, included, and proud to share their culture across generations.

What We Do

  • Host cultural events, gatherings, and celebrations
  • Share updates, resources, and community information
  • Collaborate with local groups and community partners

Our Values

These values guide how we show up for our members and the wider community.

Inclusivity

We welcome everyone and create spaces where people feel respected and safe.

Service

We support our community through outreach, information, and meaningful initiatives.

Culture

We celebrate Filipino heritage through traditions, food, music, and shared stories.

Connection

We build relationships that strengthen community and create a sense of belonging.

Land acknowledgement

We respectfully acknowledge that we are on the traditional, ancestral, and unceded territory of the Syilx Okanagan Nation.
